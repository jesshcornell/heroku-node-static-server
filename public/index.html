    About Us

    Barry’s Buns is a family owned business operated by Joel (aka Barry) and his wife, Jen. Joel utilizes his expertise in the kitchen and heads up recipe development and production, while Jen handles all marketing, brand development, event planning, and business administration.

    Joel was born a baker. He started his own cheesecake business when he was 14 years old. During high school, he took first place in a culinary competition winning a scholarship from Careers Through Culinary Arts Program (C-CAP) to attend Johnson & Wales University in Rhode Island. Joel graduated with a degree in Pastry Arts and was most recently the Regional Bakery Associate Coordinator of Whole Foods Market for the Mid-Atlantic Region. His formal career in pastry began at the Pennsylvania Convention Center and was also the Assistant Pastry Chef at The Rittenhouse Hotel under Executive Chef Jean Marie LaCroix.

    It has always been a lifelong goal for Joel to have his own place and sticky buns have always been a favorite of Joel’s since childhood. His fondest childhood memory is that of his dad regularly bringing home sticky buns for the family. Additionally, his dad, for many years before his recent passing, had his own business in a farmer's style market. Joel loved the atmosphere and helping his dad take care of the customers, so when the opportunity arose for a location within the MARKET at the Fareway (2017 Best of Philly Food Destination) in the Chestnut Hill neighborhood of Philadelphia, it was a sign that now is the time for Joel to open his own bakery.

    Our Buns

    Sticky Buns

    Barry’s Signature
    Cream Cheese Icing
    A wonderfully creamy frosting made up of butter, cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla whipped to perfection. Not too sweet but packed with flavor. Perfect addition to any of our baked goods or to eat by itself (we won’t judge!).
    Sticky Buns
    A sweet yeasted dough rolled with brown sugar and cinnamon filling topped with our signature glaze (smear). Barry’s flavors are: Walnut-Raisin, Plain, Cream Cheese Icing, Walnut, Apple Crumb, Raisin, and Seasonal.
    Sticky Bun Bread Pudding
    Now this is the best of both worlds - Barry’s sticky buns and bread pudding! Bite size pieces of our sticky buns in a rich custard baked to a golden-brown perfection. No wonder it is a best seller!
    Mini Cinnamon Rolls
    A sweet yeasted dough rolled with brown sugar and cinnamon then topped with a sugar glaze.
    Cinnamon Roll Bites
    Four petite cinnamon rolls in a “grab and go container” served with our signature cream cheese icing for dipping.
    Large Cinnamon Rolls
    A sweet yeasted dough rolled with brown sugar and cinnamon filling then iced with our signature cream cheese icing.
